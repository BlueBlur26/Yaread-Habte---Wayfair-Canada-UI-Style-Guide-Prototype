Welcome to my Wayfair Canada UI Style Guide Prototype!

The goal of this website is to ease the documentation and the usage of brand guidelines in the digital age. Designers, front-end developers, and brand managers can use this style guide to become more efficient and improve brand consistency over all channels.

The Logo

The Wayfair logo is customized using different colours such as green and pink.
This is applied for both desktop and mobile versions.

Desktop Mobile

The Grid

The grid structure is composed of one column grid, a two column grid, a three column grid, and a four column grid. This grid is very flexible and can be changed depending on the content of any website. Although the grids can be used in a variety of ways, together, they help to unify content and create a sense of consistency through a website.

Buttons

The buttons for my website consists of buttons related to Wayfair Canada. This section of the style guide cover aspects like button design, size, color, and placement to ensure a consistent and intuitive user experience. The guide outlines the visual style of buttons, including shape, borders, shadows, and gradients, as well as text styles and alignment within buttons.
